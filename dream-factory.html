DREAM FACTORY
SOUND SANCTUARY
A sanctuary for sound and creative transmission — San Diego's High Desert.
MISSION

Founded by Dustin Valentine and Chantaia Soleil, the Dream Factory Sound Sanctuary is a reflection of the empowerments they received by Sufi Master Isa Love Dragon. Their committed mission is to hold a sanctuary for artists to not only explore and experiment with sound but to reveal and transmit their unique signature frequency into the world.

Dream Factory Sound Sanctuary is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization providing musicians short-term residencies including access to world-class instruments, recording facilities, and creative mentorship.

WHAT WE OFFER
STUDIO SESSIONS
Professional recording and production in San Diego's High Desert. Engineering and creative collaboration included. $80/hr · 4-hr $300 · 8-hr $575. Sliding scale available for independent artists.
MUSIC PRODUCTION
For artists who need more than studio time. If you have the voice, the feeling, or the seed of a song — we can help build the world around it.
ARTIST RESIDENCY
3-week immersive residency. Private accommodations, meals, full studio access, wellness services, and professional video documentation. Applications open.
THE COLLECTION
