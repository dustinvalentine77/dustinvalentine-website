Dustin Valentine is a musician, producer, sound designer, and teacher based in San Diego, CA. Co-founder of Dream Factory Sound Sanctuary — a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to holding space for artists to find and transmit their unique sonic signature. Twenty years of practice across multiple lineages, contemplative traditions, and hardware synthesis, brought into one life and one body of work.
