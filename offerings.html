OFFERINGS
Here are the ways we can work together.

Every session starts with listening — to the person, to what they're carrying, to where the music wants to go. The work is different every time: some people arrive with a finished idea that needs a producer in the room, others with a half-formed sketch, others with a need to track or mix or learn. Whatever the starting point, the approach is the same — attune first, offer input only when something is clearly heard that would serve the vision. The goal is always to help make the dream a reality, not to impose one.

YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE

You Are Responsible is a 16-part awareness training created by Isa Love Dragon and Murshida Rudi. It is a practical, experiential program — not a lecture series.

SENSING
Breathing and meditation to dissolve tension and sharpen perception.
INTEGRITY
Clear communication, keeping your word, completing cycles, honoring time, space, and energy.
WITNESS
Observing your inner dialogue, releasing patterns that don't serve you, choosing ones that do.
CREATIVE PLAN
Designing a life, livelihood, or project that actually reflects who you are.

I completed this training and am available to guide individuals or small groups through it.
