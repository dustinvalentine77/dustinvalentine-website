CURIOSITIES
Things that interest me. Make of them what you will.

Around 4am one morning in 2004, near Long Beach, CA, I watched a large black triangular craft move silently across the sky directly above me for about five minutes before it disappeared from view. No sound. No running lights. No frame of reference for what it was. I searched for other sightings the next morning and found nothing. It stayed an open question for years.

About seven years later I came across Bashar, and in one of his channelings he described their craft as black and triangular. Years later, Tom DeLonge's Sekret Machines offered a different layer of confirmation — introducing the TR-3B Astra and the possibility that what I saw may have been extraterrestrial, human-made, or both — that ET technology may have been back-engineered and is quietly in use. The question shifted from whether these things are real to something more unsettling: who's flying them.

EARTHLY MYSTERIES

Megalithic structures exist all over the world. How they were built remains genuinely unknown — the official explanations have never been fully satisfying, and the debate and speculation around them continues to grow. These are some of the structures and researchers that have held my attention.

THE PYRAMIDS
Giza, Teotihuacan, and beyond. The engineering precision and astronomical alignment of ancient pyramid complexes continues to challenge conventional explanations.
SACSAYHUAMÁN
The massive Incan fortress near Cusco, Peru. Polygonal stone blocks weighing hundreds of tons, fitted with sub-millimeter precision.
OLLANTAYTAMBO
Ancient Incan ceremonial site in the Sacred Valley. Massive stone terraces and walls that suggest a level of engineering far beyond what's been officially explained.
EASTER ISLAND
The Moai statues of Rapa Nui. Who built them, how were they transported, and what do they represent?
THE SERAPEUM AT SAQQARA
An underground complex near Memphis, Egypt, housing massive granite sarcophagi weighing up to 100 tons each, machined to tolerances that remain unexplained by conventional archaeology.
THE GROWING EARTH
The hypothesis that Earth has been expanding over geological time — challenging conventional plate tectonics and reframing ancient maps and planetary history. Neal Adams produced some of the most striking visual animations of this model.
BRIEN FOERSTER
Researcher and author focused on ancient megalithic sites, elongated skulls, and lost advanced civilizations. Extensive fieldwork in Peru, Egypt, and beyond.
GRAHAM HANCOCK
GRAHAMHANCOCK.COM
Author and researcher arguing for a lost advanced civilization predating the known historical record. His work on ancient monuments, catastrophism, and altered states has been influential and controversial in equal measure.
